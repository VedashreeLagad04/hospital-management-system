Personal Data Privacy Policy

1. I hereby authorise Premium Care SG Pte. Ltd. (“PCARE”) to access, collect, use, disclose, process or otherwise handle all my personal data for the following “Purposes”:

(a) To assist me to audit and determine the scope and extent of my current medical and healthcare insurance coverage by working with such licensed financial advisers as it is required to do so;

(b) To identify my healthcare and medical needs;

(c) To assist me to procure the advice and expertise of licensed financial advisers to restructure my existing insurance coverage and/or propose further or other cost-effective insurance coverage as may be necessary;

(d) To manage and process all healthcare or medical and healthcare insurance applications and/or claims on my behalf;

(e) To identify and secure consultations with relevant medical specialists or consultants and allied healthcare professionals;

(f) To co-ordinate any specialty care arrangements, including travel, transport and lodging requirements as may be required to facilitate the procurement of medical/health-check consultation and/or treatment;

(g) To facilitate the provision of medical care and treatment from relevant medical specialists or consultants and allied healthcare professionals; and

(h) Any other purpose as may be reasonably required for the provision of the Services.

2. I hereby authorise and consent to the disclosure and/or sharing of my personal data to all such relevant persons, including but not limited to PCARE’s healthcare Administrative managers, specialist clinics, hospitals, allied healthcare professionals, financial advisers and my employer for the above mentioned Purposes only.

3. I hereby declare that any personal data I may provide to PCARE is complete, accurate and up to date at all times, and I shall promptly notify PCARE of any changes or updates, if any, to such personal data.

4. For the avoidance of doubt, “personal data” shall have the same meaning as ascribed and defined in the Protection of Personal Data Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) (“PDPA”). I hereby acknowledge that my consent is granted to PCARE to use my personal data for the provision of the Services, and/or for the Purposes, pursuant to the PDPA.

5. I further acknowledge and confirm that to facilitate the provision of the Services, and for the Purposes, PCARE may work with such licensed financial advisors as it shall deem fit and proper on my behalf or with me in order to assist me to determine the adequacy of my current medical and healthcare insurance coverage, and/or to restructure the same if need be. Towards that end, I acknowledge that PCARE shall not at any time be acting as a financial adviser to me, nor shall PCARE be providing me with any financial advisory services as prescribed under the Financial Advisers Act (Cap. 110) in so doing.
